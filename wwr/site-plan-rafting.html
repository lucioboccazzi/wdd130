Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to help customers have an easy experience preparing for the trip and getting ready to manewuver and navigate through rapids and turbulent waters. We'll help you have a great experience and also have fun while creating memories in a safe enviroment.

Audience

Who are our target customers? We aim to reach adults from the ages 20 to 30 that are in good physical shape that allows them to go through the experience. What do we want? We want to help you understand and decide if you're ready and capable of going through the experience before trying. How will my audience access the site? They will be able to log in using any mobile device or computer.
